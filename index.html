Edge-Replacement Fractal Settings (click + drag here)






This fractal generator works by taking several fractal "nodes" and connecting them together into a series of vectors (This series is the "replacer"). A copy of the replacer is made, and each vector in that copy is replaced with the replacer, such that the replacer retains its original direction and magnitude. Each vector in that series of vectors is replaced with the replacer. This process repeats a number of times equal to the iteration count. At least three fractal nodes are required to create a fractal.
